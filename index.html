Full Stack Development

Full stack development refers to the ability to work on both the front end and back end of a web application. A full stack developer handles everything from designing user interfaces (using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) to managing databases, server-side logic, APIs, and deployment (using technologies like Node.js, Python, PHP, MySQL, MongoDB, etc.). They understand how all layers of a system interact and can build a complete application independently. Full stack development is valuable in startups and agile teams where versatility and broad technical knowledge are essential to build, test, and deploy software efficiently across the entire stack.
